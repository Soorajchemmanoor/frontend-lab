Top 5 Places to Visit in India

  • Agra - Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Jaipur - Known as the Pink City, famous for its forts, palaces, and vibrant culture.
  • Kerala - Renowned for its backwaters, lush greenery, and Ayurvedic traditions.
  • Varanasi - One of the world's oldest living cities, sacred to Hindus and full of spiritual significance.
  • Leh-Ladakh - A high-altitude desert with breathtaking landscapes, monasteries, and adventure sports.
